The Bridge and the Torch:

First Question:

* Four people need to cross a rickety bridge at night. They have only one torch, and the bridge can only hold two people at a time. Person A can cross in 1 minute, B in 2 minutes, C in 5 minutes, and D in 8 minutes. When two people cross together, they must go at the slower person's pace. (What's the fastest way for all four to cross?)
